# Accessibility Staff Training Packet

**Role:** Accessibility Staff/Volunteer  
**Convention:** ______________________  

## Part 1: The Mission
- **Goal:** Ensure no attendee is excluded due to disability.
- **Approach:** Proactive, warm, dignified.
- **Rule:** Believe the attendee. If they say they need help, they do.

## Part 2: Key Roles
- **Director:** Overall lead, escalation point.
- **Staff:** Intake, desk coverage, floor patrol.
- **Volunteers:** Support, directions, equipment help.

## Part 3: Common Scenarios
### Scenario A: Attendee asks for a wheelchair
1.  Ask: "How far do you need to go?"
2.  Check inventory.
3.  Demonstrate safe use.
4.  Log the loan (Time Out, Attendee ID, Item).
5.  Follow up at end of day.

### Scenario B: Attendee seems overwhelmed
1.  Approach calmly: "Hi, I'm with accessibility. Can I help?"
2.  Offer quiet room: "We have a quiet space nearby."
3.  Do not touch without asking.
4.  If they decline, respect it. "Okay, I'm here if you need me."

### Scenario C: Attendee has a service dog
1.  Do not pet the dog.
2.  Do not ask about the disability.
3.  Only ask: "Is this a service animal required for a disability?" and "What task is it trained for?"
4.  Direct other attendees who complain to you.

## Part 4: Emergency Protocols
- **Medical Emergency:** Call Safety immediately.
- **Distressed Attendee:** Quiet room first. If unsafe, call Safety.
- **Lost Equipment:** Log it, notify Director.
