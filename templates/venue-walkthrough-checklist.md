## 1. Entrances & Exits
- [ ] Main entrance has automatic doors or ramp?
- [ ] All emergency exits are accessible (no stairs only)?
- [ ] Clear signage to accessible entrances?
- [ ] Parking lot has accessible spots near entrance?

## 2. Pathways & Navigation
- [ ] Corridors are wide enough for wheelchairs (min 36")?
- [ ] No temporary obstacles (tables, displays) blocking paths?
- [ ] Flooring is smooth (no thick carpet that traps wheels)?
- [ ] Elevators are located near high-traffic areas?
- [ ] Elevator buttons are at accessible height?

## 3. Programming Spaces
- [ ] Accessible seating areas identified in each room?
- [ ] Sight lines from accessible seats are clear?
- [ ] Microphone stands are adjustable height?
- [ ] ASL interpreter placement has clear view?
- [ ] Aisles remain clear for mobility aid movement?

## 4. Restrooms
- [ ] ADA-compliant restrooms located on every floor?
- [ ] Signs clearly mark accessible restrooms?
- [ ] Grab bars are secure?
- [ ] Sinks are accessible (no cabinets underneath)?

## 5. Accessibility Desk Location
- [ ] Location is visible from main flow?
- [ ] No stairs to reach the desk?
- [ ] Adequate space for queueing?
- [ ] Power outlet available?
- [ ] Quiet enough for private conversations?

## 6. Quiet Room Candidate
- [ ] Room is away from main noise sources?
- [ ] Room has adjustable lighting or blackout capability?
- [ ] Room has comfortable seating (couch/chairs)?
- [ ] Room is accessible (no stairs)?
- [ ] Camera placement is feasible (IT check)?

## 7. Food & Amenities
- [ ] Food vendors have accessible counters?
- [ ] Water fountains are accessible?
- [ ] Clear path to food areas from programming?
