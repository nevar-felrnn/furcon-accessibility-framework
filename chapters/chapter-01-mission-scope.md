## Chapter 1: Mission & Scope

The accessibility department exists to ensure that no attendee has difficulty participating in any aspect of the convention. This includes but is not limited to retrieving their badge, attending events in any programming space, accessing dealer areas, and moving freely throughout the venue.

The department serves attendees with visible and invisible disabilities, including mobility, sensory, hearing, vision, and cognitive needs. The department approaches its work proactively — staff are trained to identify attendees who may benefit from support and to offer assistance warmly, without assumption, and without waiting to be asked.

Emergency response remains the responsibility of the convention's safety team, with accessibility available for consultation as needed.

This framework is a living document. As the department encounters new needs, the knowledge gained is recorded and used to improve future conventions.
